Facts About Jamacia!

As one of the most diverse places in the world, visiting Jamaica is a fascinating learning experience. Adventure: The island of rhythm and sway is also the island of adventure. Come and explore our top spots. Food: From jerk to patties to world-famous ice cream, Jamaica is a destination for foodies.

Easy going reggae tunes will make you jump out of your chair; you’ll dance and sing as a real Jamaican soon. When your eyes catch dancehall moves, you don’t need class at all; shake your body and let yourself go. Rastafari Nyabinghi drum and chanting ceremonies will bring you in a meditative mood. Empty your mind and surrender to the heartbeat of the drum. Feel peace and harmony as Jamaica is the place of one love and unity.
